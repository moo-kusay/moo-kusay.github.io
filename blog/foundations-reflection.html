Foundations Reflection

2/10/2022

My feelings...

Wow, I can't believe the foundation fly by so quick and I am sure other students felt the same as well. My overall feeling and what I felt is that I can do so much better than the last five weeks, I felt I let myself down, my fellow students down, which I could assist with a lot of things; however I am glad, happy, and proud to see that fellow students across the country are engaging, helping, and supporting one another, wanting the best for another. Great mahi amongst the students, and staffs.

Last but not least, I felt like I let my lovely facilitators down, I know can do so much more, and I hope to take the experiences the last five weeks, and how I felt into the bootcamp, and in life to do better, be better, not only for myself but others, individuals I met a long this journey of life.

Nevertheless, I am grateful, optimistic, and excited for the bootcamp, and for growth, and so on.

That we're missing this in our traditional school curriculum and that it should get implemented, students in our public school get throw together in public system without a tool or tools that actually teach them to manage their emotions, or get taught about how their brain work, and more importatly how to learn (learning how to learn), how to be self aware, and have empathy towards their fellow students, and themselves.
